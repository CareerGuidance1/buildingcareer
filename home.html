  • Informed Decision-Making:

  • Skill Assessment and Development:

    • Providing guidance on crafting effective resumes, cover letters, and job applications to enhance job prospects.

  • Interview Preparation:

    • Offering tips, strategies, and mock interview sessions to help individuals prepare confidently for job interviews.

  • Networking Opportunities:

    • Facilitating connections with professionals, alumni, and industry experts to expand individuals' professional networks.
